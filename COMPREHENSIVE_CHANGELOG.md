   - Divination rituals for the coming year

   - "Dziady" means "grandfathers" or "forefathers"
   - Families commune with deceased ancestors
   - Food offerings (kutia, honey, bread) for wandering souls
   - Empty places set at tables
   - Welcoming ancestors as nature awakens

4. **Autumn Dziady** - Forefathers' Eve (Oct 31 - Nov 2)
   - Most important Dziady celebration
   - Boundary between worlds is thinnest
   - Bonfires guide spirits home
   - Ritual feasts (stypa) honor the dead
   - Immortalized in Mickiewicz's romantic drama
   - Merged with Catholic All Saints' and All Souls' Days

5. **Spring Equinox (Jare Gody)** - March 20
   - "Jare Gody" means "Spring Feast"
   - Perfect balance between light and dark
   - Burning/drowning Marzanna (winter/death effigy)
   - Welcoming Lada (spring/life goddess)
   - Egg decoration (pisanki) predates Christianity
